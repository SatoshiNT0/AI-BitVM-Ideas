# AI + BitVM: Fresh Ideas for the Future of Bitcoin and Artificial Intelligence

## Introduction
BitVM enables Turing-complete computation on Bitcoin without changing the consensus rules. Combined with **AI**, it opens revolutionary possibilities: on-chain AI model verification, secure autonomous agents, distributed training, and more.

This repository collects innovative ideas, conceptual prototypes, and opportunities for 2026 and beyond.

## Main Ideas

### 1. AI Inference Verification on Bitcoin (BitVM + ZK)
- Use BitVM to verify AI model inference proofs directly on the Bitcoin blockchain.
- Applications: Tamper-proof AI oracles, verifiable market predictions.
- Advantage: Trustless execution.

### 2. Autonomous AI Agents on Bitcoin
- Agents that execute BitVM smart contracts for trading, DeFi, or DAOs.
- Security: Execution verified on Bitcoin, reducing L2 attack surfaces.

### 3. Distributed AI Model Training with BitVM
- Decentralized compute network where nodes contribute GPU/CPU power and BitVM verifies training progress.
- Bitcoin-secured alternative to centralized cloud providers.

### 4. AI for BitVM Optimization
- AI models that automatically generate and optimize BitVM programs.
- ML-powered vulnerability detection in BitVM contracts.

### 5. AI Compute Marketplace on Bitcoin
- Marketplace for renting compute for AI inference/training, paid in BTC and verified via BitVM.

### 6. BitVM for Edge Device AI Model Updates
- Lightweight verification of AI model updates on IoT devices using BitVM.

### 7. Advanced Prediction Markets Powered by AI
- AI-driven oracles with BitVM verification for automatic market resolution.
