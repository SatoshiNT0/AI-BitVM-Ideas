# AI Inference Verification with BitVM

**Fresh 2026 Idea**: Allow anyone to verify that an AI model produced a specific output without revealing the full model (using ZK proofs + BitVM).

**Benefits**:
- Trustworthy AI oracles for DeFi and prediction markets.
- On-chain "reasoning" proofs from large language models.
- Strong resistance to censorship and manipulation.

**Technical Challenges**: Optimize the dispute game for BitVM's one-round challenge model and minimize on-chain costs.
